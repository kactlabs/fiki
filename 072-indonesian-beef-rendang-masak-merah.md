# Indonesian Beef Rendang Masak Merah (Spicy Beef Rendang)

Indonesian beef rendang is a rich and flavorful dish from the Minangkabau region of Indonesia, known for its bold flavors and spicy kick.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 500g **beef shoulder or brisket**, cut into bite-sized pieces

### Spice Blend
- 2 **tablespoons** coriander seeds
- 1 **tablespoon** cumin seeds
- 1 **tablespoon** cinnamon powder
- 2 **teaspoons** ground ginger
- 2 **teaspoons** turmeric powder
- 1 **teaspoon** cayenne pepper

### Aromatics
- 4 ** cloves** garlic, minced
- 2 **inches** piece of fresh ginger, peeled and sliced
- 2 **sticks** lemongrass, bruised

### Coconut Milk
- 400ml **coconut milk**
- 200ml **water**

### Acidic Elements
- 2 **limes**, cut into wedges
- 1/4 **cup** palm sugar
- 2 **tablespoons** tamarind paste

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Spice Blend
Grind the coriander seeds, cumin seeds, cinnamon powder, ginger, turmeric powder, and cayenne pepper in a spice grinder until well combined.

### 2. Roast the Aromatics
