# Japanese Teriyaki Chicken Katsu Buns ([Japanese Street Food])

Bite-sized, crispy buns filled with juicy chicken and savory teriyaki sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat and Poultry
- 500g **pork loin or shoulder**, sliced into thin strips
- 250g **chicken breast**, cut into small pieces
- 2 **eggs**
- 1 **cup all-purpose flour**

### Vegetables
- 1/2 cup **grated daikon radish**
- 1/4 cup **finely chopped scallions (green onions)**
- 2 cloves **garlic, minced**
- 1/4 cup **sliced shiitake mushrooms**

### Sauces and Marinades
- 1/2 cup **teriyaki sauce** (see below for recipe)
- 2 tablespoons **soy sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **rice vinegar**
- 1 tablespoon **granulated sugar**
- 1 teaspoon **ground ginger**
- 1 teaspoon **ground white pepper**

### Buns
- 4-6 **brioche buns**, cut in half horizontally

---

## Teriyaki Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **soy sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **granulated sugar**
- 2 tablespoons **rice vinegar**
- 2 tablespoons **water**
- 1 tablespoon **grated fresh ginger**
- 1 teaspoon **ground white pepper**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Meat and Marinade
In a large bowl, whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, ginger, and white pepper. Add the sliced pork and chicken pieces, making sure they are coated evenly with the marinade. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

### 2. Prepare the Filling
In a separate bowl, whisk together daikon radish, scallions, garlic, and shiitake mushrooms. Set aside.

### 3. Prepare the Breading Station
In three shallow dishes, place the flour, eggs, and panko breadcrumbs (if using). Season the flour with salt.

### 4. Bread the Meat and Vegetables
Dredge the marinated meat pieces in the flour mixture, shaking off excess. Dip the floured meat in the beaten egg, making sure it's fully coated. Dredge the egg-coated meat in the panko
