# Bengali-Style Fish Curry (Bengal, India)

A flavorful fish curry dish originating from the Bengal region in Eastern India.

---

## Ingredients

### [Protein]
- 1 **lb** **fresh fish fillets** (such as cod or haddock)
- 2 **pieces** **fresh ginger**
- 2 **cloves** **whole cinnamon stick**

### [Aromatics]
- 2 **medium onions**, chopped
- 3 ** cloves** **garlic**, minced
- 1 **teaspoon** **ground cumin**
- 1 **teaspoon** **curry powder**

### [Spices and Seasonings]
- 1/2 **teaspoon** **turmeric powder**
- 1/2 **teaspoon** **red chili powder**
- Salt, to taste
- 1/4 **cup** **fresh cilantro**, chopped

### [Sauce Ingredients]
- 1/2 **cup** **coconut milk**
- 2 **tablespoons** **vegetable oil**

### [Additional Ingredients]
- 2 **medium tomatoes**, diced (optional)
- Fresh lime wedges, for serving
