# Peruvian Ceviche Clásico (Peruvian Raw Fish Dish)

A refreshing and flavorful dish from Peru, made with raw fish marinated in citrus juices, mixed with onions, peppers, and aji amarillo peppers.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 1 pound **fresh white fish** (such as halibut or sea bass), cut into small pieces

### Produce
- 2 **limes**, juiced
- 1/4 cup **extra virgin olive oil**
- 1 large **red onion**, thinly sliced
- 2 **aji amarillo peppers**, seeded and chopped
- 2 **tomatoes**, diced
- 1/4 cup **fresh cilantro** leaves, chopped

### Miscellaneous
- Salt to taste
- 1 teaspoon **Peruvian aji amarillo pepper paste**
- 1 tablespoon **sugar**

---

## Ceviche Sauce (Lemon and Aji Amarillo Paste)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **lemon juice**
- 1 tablespoon **aji amarillo pepper paste**
- 1 tablespoon **olive oil**
- Salt to taste

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Aji Amarillo Pepper Paste
Grind 2 aji amarillo peppers and 1 teaspoon of salt in a blender or food processor until you get a smooth paste.

### 2. Marinate the Fish
In a large bowl, combine fish pieces, lime juice, olive oil, chopped onion, chopped cilantro, salt to taste, and aji amarillo pepper paste. Mix well and let marinate for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.

### 3. Combine All Ingredients
Add diced tomatoes and the prepared aji amarillo pepper paste mixture to the fish marinade. Stir well and adjust seasoning if needed.

---

## Optional: Variation Name (Spicy Ceviche)

- Add more chopped aji amarillo peppers or hot sauce like sriracha for extra heat
