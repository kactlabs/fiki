# Baidaoush (Egyptian Ful Medames with Garlic and Lemon)

Baidaoush is a popular Egyptian side dish made from fava beans, garlic, and lemon juice. It's often served with pita bread or rice.

## Ingredients

### Category 1
- 1 cup dried fava beans, soaked overnight and drained
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons olive oil

### Category 2
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon chopped scallions (green onions)
- 1/4 cup grated freshly squeezed orange zest

## Baidaoush Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
