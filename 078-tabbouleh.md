# Tabbouleh (Middle Eastern Salad)

Tabbouleh is a traditional Middle Eastern salad made with bulgur wheat, parsley, tomatoes, mint, and lemon juice.

## Ingredients

### Vegetables
- 1 cup **bulgur wheat**
- 2 cups **chopped fresh parsley**
- 1 cup **chopped fresh mint**
- 1 cup **diced tomatoes**

### Dressing
- 2 tablespoons **lemon juice**
- 1 tablespoon **olive oil**
- 1 teaspoon **salt**
- 1/2 teaspoon **black pepper**

---

## Tabbouleh Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 1/4 cup **chopped fresh parsley**
- 1/4 cup **chopped fresh mint**
- 2 tablespoons **lemon juice**
- 1 tablespoon **olive oil**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Cooking Bulgur Wheat
Rinse the bulgur wheat in a fine mesh strainer until the water runs clear. Drain well and set aside.

### 2. Chopping Vegetables
Chop the fresh parsley, mint, and tomatoes into small pieces.

### 3. Mixing Tabbouleh Sauce
Combine the chopped herbs, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and black pepper in a bowl. Mix well to combine.

### 4. Assembling Tabbouleh
In a large serving dish, combine the cooked bulgur wheat, chopped parsley, mint, and tomatoes.

### 5. Adding Tabbouleh Sauce
Pour the prepared tabbouleh sauce over the salad and toss gently to coat.
