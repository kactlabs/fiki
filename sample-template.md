# Bibimbap (Korean Mixed Rice Bowl)

Bibimbap means **"mixed rice"** in Korean. It is a bowl of rice topped with vegetables, protein, egg, and gochujang (Korean chili paste). Everything is mixed before eating.

---

## Ingredients

### Base
- 2 cups **cooked short-grain rice**

### Vegetables
- 1 cup **spinach**
- 1 cup **bean sprouts**
- 1 **carrot** (julienned)
- 1 **zucchini** (julienned)
- 4–5 **mushrooms** (sliced)
- 1/2 cup **kimchi** (optional)

### Protein (choose one)
- 150 g **thin sliced beef (bulgogi style)**  
OR  
- **tofu** for vegetarian

### Egg
- 1 **fried egg (sunny side up)**

### Seasoning
- 2 tbsp **soy sauce**
- 1 tbsp **sesame oil**
- 1 tbsp **sesame seeds**
- 1 tbsp **minced garlic**
- salt to taste

---

## Bibimbap Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 tbsp **gochujang**
- 1 tsp **sesame oil**
- 1 tsp **sugar or honey**
- 1 tsp **rice vinegar**
- 1 tsp **water**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Cook the Rice
Cook short-grain rice using a rice cooker or pot.  
Keep it warm.

---

### 2. Prepare Vegetables
Cook vegetables separately with a little oil and salt.

- **Spinach**: blanch 30 seconds, squeeze water, mix with sesame oil and garlic.
- **Bean sprouts**: boil 2 minutes and lightly salt.
- **Carrot & zucchini**: stir fry 2–3 minutes.
- **Mushrooms**: sauté with garlic and soy sauce.

---

### 3. Cook the Protein
For **beef**:
1. Marinate beef with soy sauce, garlic, and sesame oil.
2. Stir fry for 2–3 minutes.

For vegetarian:
- pan fry **tofu cubes**.

---

### 4. Fry the Egg
Cook a **sunny-side-up egg** so the yolk stays runny.

---

### 5. Assemble the Bowl
1. Place hot rice in a bowl.
2. Arrange vegetables around the bowl in sections.
3. Add beef or tofu.
4. Place fried egg in the center.
5. Add kimchi if using.

---

### 6. Add Sauce and Mix
1. Add 1–2 tbsp bibimbap sauce.
2. Sprinkle sesame seeds.
3. Mix everything together before eating.

---

## Optional: Dolsot Bibimbap (Stone Bowl Version)

- Heat a **stone bowl** with sesame oil.
- Add rice and cook slightly so the bottom becomes crispy.
- Add toppings and egg on top.
- Serve sizzling.
