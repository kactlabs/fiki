# Jamaican Jerk Chicken ([Jamaican and Caribbean cuisine])

Traditional jerk seasoning from Jamaica, marinated in a spicy allspice and thyme blend.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat Category
- 2 lbs **Boneless, skinless chicken thighs**
- 1/4 cup **brown sugar**

### Spice Blend Category
- 3 tbsp **allspice powder**
- 2 tsp **thyme leaves**
- 2 tsp **cinnamon powder**
- 1 tsp **ground ginger**
- 1 tsp **paprika**
- 1/2 tsp **cayenne pepper**
- 1/2 tsp **salt**
- 1/4 tsp **black pepper**

### Additional Ingredient Category
- 2 tbsp **vegetable oil**
- 2 cloves **garlic, minced**
- 1 Scotch bonnet pepper, seeded and chopped (optional)

---

## Jerk Seasoning Sauce

Mix the following:

- 1/4 cup **soy sauce**
- 2 tbsp **brown sugar**
- 2 tbsp **lime juice**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Preparing the Spice Blend
Combine allspice powder, thyme leaves, cinnamon powder, ginger, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper in a small bowl.

### 2. Marinating the Chicken
In a large bowl, combine chicken thighs, brown sugar, vegetable oil, garlic, and Scotch bonnet pepper (if using). Mix well to coat the chicken evenly. Sprinkle the jerk spice blend over the chicken and mix again until the chicken is evenly coated.

---

### 3. Grilling or Baking the Chicken
Preheat grill or oven to medium-high heat. Place the marinated chicken thighs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or directly on the grill grates. Cook for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.

---

## Optional: Jerk Chicken Glaze

- Mix the following:

    - 2 tbsp **soy sauce**
    - 1 tbsp **brown sugar**
    - 2 tbsp **lime juice**

    Brush the jerk chicken glaze over the grilled or baked chicken during the last 5 minutes of cooking for an extra layer of flavor.
