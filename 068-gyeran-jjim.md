# Gyeran-jjim (Korean Steamed Egg Custard)

Gyeran-jjim is a traditional Korean steamed egg custard dish, often served as a side or used as an ingredient in various Korean dishes.

## Ingredients

### Proteins and Dairy
- 1/2 cup **large eggs**
- 1/2 cup **heavy cream**

### Flavorings and Spices
- 1 tsp **ground white pepper**
- 1 tsp **salt**
- 1 tsp **dried seaweed flakes (gulimja)**

### Garnishes and Extras
- Chopped green onions for garnish
- Sesame seeds for garnish

## Gyeran-jjim Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 tbsp **soy sauce**
- 2 tbsp **ginger juice**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Preparing the Custard
In a blender or food processor, combine eggs, heavy cream, white pepper, salt, and seaweed flakes. Blend until smooth.

### 2. Steaming the Custard
Transfer the blended mixture to a heatproof container or steamer bowl. Steam over boiling water for about 40-45 minutes, or until the custard is set but still slightly jiggly in the center.

### 3. Cooling and Serving
Remove the container from the steamer and let it cool to room temperature. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds before serving.

---

## Optional: Spicy Gyeran-jjim

- To make a spicy version, add 1-2 tsp **gochugaru (Korean red pepper flakes)** to the blended mixture before steaming.
