# Shish Taouk Lebanese Chicken Skewers ([Origin: Middle Eastern Cuisine])

A classic Lebanese street food dish featuring marinated chicken skewers, served with a side of creamy tahini sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Marinating Mixture
- 1 cup **plain Greek yogurt**
- 2 cloves **garlic, minced**
- 1 tablespoon **lemon juice**
- 1 teaspoon **paprika**
- 1/2 teaspoon **cumin**
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 10 boneless chicken breast or thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces

### Tahini Sauce
- 1 cup **tahini paste**
- 1/4 cup **plain Greek yogurt**
- 2 tablespoons **lemon juice**
- 1 minced **garlic clove**
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Garnish
- Chopped fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish
- Sliced radishes or red onion for garnish (optional)

---

## Shish Taouk Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **pomegranate molasses**
- 1 tablespoon **lemon juice**
- 1 minced **garlic clove**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Marinate the Chicken
In a large bowl, whisk together the marinating mixture ingredients. Add the chicken pieces and mix until they are evenly coated with the marinade. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

---

### 2. Prepare the Skewers
Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. Brush the grill or grill pan with oil to prevent sticking.

---

### 3. Grill the Chicken
Grill the chicken skewers for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are cooked through and slightly charred.

---

### 4. Make the Tahini Sauce
In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini sauce ingredients until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

---

### 5. Serve
Serve the grilled chicken skewers hot or warm with the creamy tahini sauce drizzled on top. Garnish with chopped parsley or cilantro, sliced radishes, or red onion if desired.

---
