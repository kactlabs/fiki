# Jamaican Jerk Chicken ([Traditional Caribbean dish])

A classic Jamaican dish with a bold, aromatic flavor profile.

---

## Ingredients

### Marinating Category
- 1/2 cup **allspice**
- 1/4 cup **thyme**
- 1/4 cup **scotch bonnet peppers**, chopped (use gloves when handling)
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **ginger**, grated
- 2 tablespoons **lime juice**

### Rubbing Category
- 1/2 cup **brown sugar**
- 2 tablespoons **allspice**
- 1 teaspoon **cinnamon**
- 1 teaspoon **nutmeg**

### Additional ingredients
- 4 pounds **chicken breast or thighs**, cut into 8 pieces
- 2 tablespoons **vegetable oil**

---

## Jerk Chicken Sauce

Mix the following:

- 1/4 cup **soy sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **lime juice**
- 1 tablespoon **brown sugar**
- 1 teaspoon **ground cinnamon**
- 1/2 teaspoon **cayenne pepper**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Marinade
In a blender or food processor, combine marinating ingredients and blend until smooth.

### 2. Marinate the Chicken
Place chicken pieces in a large bowl or zip-top plastic bag. Pour marinade over the chicken and massage to coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

---

### 3. Prepare the Rub
In a small bowl, mix together rubbing ingredients until well combined.

### 4. Apply the Rub and Oil
Remove chicken from marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. In a large bowl, combine rub and oil. Add chicken to the bowl and toss to coat evenly.

---

### 5. Grill or Roast the Chicken
Preheat grill to medium-high heat or oven to 400°F (200°C). Place chicken on the grill or in a baking dish and cook for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through, basting with pan juices every 10 minutes.

---

## Optional: Curry Jerk Chicken

- To add a curry twist, mix 1/4 cup **curry powder** into the rub before applying to the chicken.
