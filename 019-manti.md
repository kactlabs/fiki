# Manti ([Uzbek/Traditional Central Asian])

Manti are traditional Uzbek steamed dumplings filled with meat, served with a side of sour cream and onions.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat Filling
- 1 pound **ground beef**
- 1/2 cup **onion**, finely chopped
- 1/4 cup **parsley**, finely chopped
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **salt**
- 1 teaspoon **black pepper**

### Dipping Sauce
- 1 cup **sour cream**
- 2 tablespoons **chopped scallions**
- 1 tablespoon **apple cider vinegar**

### Steaming Liquid
- 4 cups **water**
- 2 tablespoons **vegetable oil**

---

## Manti Sauce (optional)

Mix the following:

- 1/4 cup **kashkaval cheese**, crumbled
- 2 tablespoons **dried mint**, chopped

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare Meat Filling
In a large mixing bowl, combine ground beef, onion, parsley, garlic, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are fully incorporated.

---

### 2. Assemble Manti
Divide the meat filling into small balls, about 1 tablespoon each. Wrap each ball in a thin layer of dough (see below for dough recipe). Make sure to seal the edges tightly.

---

### 3. Prepare Dough
In a separate mixing bowl, combine:
- 2 cups **all-purpose flour**
- 1/4 teaspoon **salt**
- 1/4 cup **vegetable oil**

Mix well until dough forms. Knead for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.

---

### 4. Steam Manti
Place manti in a steamer basket, leaving space between each dumpling. Place the steamer basket over boiling water, cover with a lid, and steam for 10-12 minutes, or until cooked through.

---

## Optional: Manti Variation

To add extra flavor, serve manti with a fried onion topping (see below).
