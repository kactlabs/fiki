# Gado-Gado ([Indonesian Salad])

Gado-Gado is a popular Indonesian salad made from mixed vegetables, tofu, and tempeh, topped with peanut sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Vegetables
- 1 cup **cabbage**, shredded
- 1 cup **carrots**, peeled and grated
- 1 cup **bean sprouts**
- 1 cup **long beans**, cut into 2-inch pieces

### Protein
- 3 oz **firm tofu**, cut into small cubes
- 3 oz **tempeh**, crumbled

### Peanut Sauce
- 1/2 cup **peanut butter**
- 1/4 cup **coconut milk**
- 2 tbsp **soy sauce**
- 1 tsp **grated ginger**
- 1 tsp **lime juice**

### Additional
- 2 tbsp **fried shallots**, chopped
- 2 kaffir lime leaves

---

## Peanut Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tbsp **peanut butter**
- 1 tbsp **coconut milk**
- 1/4 tsp **grated ginger**
- 1/4 tsp **lime juice**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare Vegetables
Chop all the vegetables into desired sizes and set aside.

### 2. Cook Tofu and Tempeh
In a small pan, heat 1 tbsp of oil over medium heat. Add tofu and tempeh and cook until golden brown on all sides.

### 3. Assemble Salad
In a large bowl, combine cooked tofu, tempeh, vegetables, peanut sauce (if using), fried shallots, and kaffir lime leaves.

---

## Optional: Gado-Gado Variation with Sambal

- To add an extra layer of flavor, mix 1/4 cup **sambal** into the peanut sauce for a spicy kick.
