# Bulgogi ([Korean BBQ Description])

Bulgogi is a popular Korean dish that originated from the royal court of Joseon Dynasty. It's a marinated beef recipe, typically made with thinly sliced ribeye or sirloin, grilled to perfection and served with kimchi and steamed rice.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 1 pound **thinly sliced ribeye steak**
- 2 tablespoons **gochujang** (Korean chili paste)

### Marinades
- 2 tablespoons **soy sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **brown sugar**
- 2 cloves **minced garlic**
- 1 tablespoon **ground ginger**
- 1 tablespoon **rice vinegar**

### Vegetables
- 2 cups **sliced zucchini**
- 2 cups **sliced carrots**
- 1 cup **green onions**, thinly sliced

### Additional ingredients
- 2 tablespoons **sesame oil**
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 2 cups **kimchi** (traditional Korean fermented cabbage)

---

## Bulgogi Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **gochujang**
- 1 tablespoon **soy sauce**
- 1 tablespoon **brown sugar**
- 1 tablespoon **rice vinegar**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Preparing the marinade
In a large bowl, whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, and rice vinegar.

### 2. Marinating the beef
Add thinly sliced ribeye steak to the marinade mixture, making sure all sides of the meat are coated evenly. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

---

### 3. Preparing vegetables
In a separate bowl, whisk together sesame oil, salt, and black pepper. Add sliced zucchini, carrots, and green onions, tossing to coat evenly.

---

### 4. Grilling the beef
Preheat grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Remove the beef from marinade, letting excess liquid drip off. Grill for 3-5 minutes per side, or until cooked to desired level of doneness.

---

### 5. Assembling Bulgogi
Transfer grilled beef to a serving platter or individual plates. Arrange kimchi and vegetables around the meat in a single layer. Serve immediately.

---

## Optional: Spicy variation

- For an extra kick, add 1 teaspoon **Gochugaru** (Korean chili flakes
