# Bhuna Masala (Indian Spicy Mutton Curry)

Bhuna Masala is a popular Indian dish made with mutton cooked in a spicy and flavorful tomato-based curry sauce, often served with basmati rice or naan bread.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 500g boneless lamb shoulder or neck, cut into small pieces

### Aromatics
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 inches ginger, peeled and grated

### Spices
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp garam masala powder
- 1 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tsp amchur powder (dried mango powder)
- 1/2 tsp ghee or vegetable oil

### Sauce
- 2 cups tomato puree
- 1 cup water
- 2 tbsp tamarind paste
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- Salt, to taste

### Garnish
- Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish
- Lemon wedges, for serving (optional)

---

## Bhuna Masala Sauce

Mix the following:

- 1/4 cup ghee or vegetable oil
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1/2 tsp red chili powder
- Salt, to taste

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Roast the Spices and Aromatics
Heat 1 tbsp ghee or oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds, coriander seeds, and cinnamon stick. Let them sizzle for a few seconds. Remove from heat.

### 2. Sear the Meat
In another pan, heat 1 tbsp ghee or oil over medium-high heat. Add lamb pieces and sear until browned on all sides. Remove from heat and set aside.

### 3. Soften the Onions
Reduce heat to low and add more ghee or oil if needed. Add chopped onions and sauté until they are lightly browned and soft, stirring occasionally.
