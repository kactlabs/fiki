# Peruvian Anticuchos (Grilled Beef Heart Skewers)

Peruvian anticuchos are a popular street food made from grilled beef heart skewers, typically served as an appetizer or snack.

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 500g beef heart
- 20g vegetable oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon grated ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Marinade
- 250ml red wine vinegar
- 125ml soy sauce
- 50g brown sugar
- 25g chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons grated lime zest

### Roasted Peppers
- 4 roasted red peppers, peeled and sliced
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt to taste

### Chimichurri Sauce (optional)
Mix the following:
- 250ml fresh parsley leaves and stems
- 125ml fresh oregano leaves
- 100g red onion, finely chopped
- 50g red pepper flakes
- 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon olive oil

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Beef Heart
Cut the beef heart into thin strips and remove any excess fat or connective tissue.

### 2. Marinate the Beef Heart
In a large bowl, whisk together the marinade ingredients. Add the beef heart strips and mix to coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

### 3. Grill the Beef Heart Skewers
Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated beef heart onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. Brush the grill with vegetable oil to prevent sticking. Grill the anticuchos for 3-4 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

### 4. Prepare the Chimichurri Sauce (optional)
Combine all chimichurri sauce ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow flavors to meld.

### 5. Assemble the Anticuchos
Once the anticuchos are grilled, brush with roasted peppers and parsley. Serve immediately, garnished with additional roasted peppers if desired.

---

## Optional: Spicy Anticuchos

- To add a spicy kick, sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the beef heart skewers before grilling.
