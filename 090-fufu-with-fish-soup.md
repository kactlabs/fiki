# Fufu with Fish Soup (Central African)

Fufu is a staple dish in Central Africa, made from boiled and pounded cassava or yams, often served with soups.

## Ingredients

### Main Dish
- 2 pounds **cassava**
- 4 cups **water**

### Fish Soup
- 1 pound **fish fillets (such as tilapia)**
- 2 tablespoons **vegetable oil**
- 1 onion **chopped**
- 3 cloves **garlic minced**
- 1 teaspoon **ground cumin**
- 1 teaspoon **paprika**
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Additional ingredients
- 2 cups **fish broth**
- 2 cups **water**

---

## Fufu with Fish Soup Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **vegetable oil**
- 2 cloves **garlic minced**
- 1 tablespoon **freshly squeezed lime juice**
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare Fufu
Boil cassava in water until tender, then pound into a dough using a mortar and pestle or a wooden mallet.

### 2. Cook Fish Soup
Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; sauté until softened. Add cumin and paprika; cook for 1 minute. Add fish broth and water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and add fish fillets; simmer until cooked through.

### 3. Prepare Fufu Serving
Divide fufu dough into small portions. Shape each portion into a ball and flatten slightly.

### 4. Serve Fufu with Fish Soup
Serve fufu balls in bowls and break them apart to serve with fish soup.
