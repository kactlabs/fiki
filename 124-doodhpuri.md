# Doodhpuri (Bengali-Style Fried Doughnuts)

Doodhpuri is a popular Bengali snack that consists of deep-fried doughnuts coated in a sweet syrup made from jaggery and sugar.

## Ingredients

### Dry Ingredients
- 2 cups **all-purpose flour**
- 1/2 cup **semolina flour**
- 1/4 teaspoon **salt**

### Wet Ingredients
- 1/4 cup **ghee or oil**
- 1/2 cup **milk**
- 1 large **egg**
- 1 tablespoon **vinegar**

### Jaggery Syrup
- 1 cup **jaggery powder**
- 1 cup **granulated sugar**
- 2 cups **water**

## Doodhpuri Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **ghee or oil**
- 2 tablespoons **grated jaggery**
- 2 tablespoons **sugar**

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Mixing the Dough
In a large mixing bowl, combine the dry ingredients and mix well. In a separate bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients until smooth. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and knead for about 10 minutes until a smooth dough forms.

### 2. Resting the Dough
Cover the dough with plastic wrap or a damp cloth and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.

### 3. Frying the Doughnuts
Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, pinch small pieces of dough into the oil to form dumplings. Fry until golden brown, then remove with a slotted spoon.

### 4. Soaking in Syrup
Dip fried doughnuts into the jaggery syrup and coat evenly.

## Optional: Chopped Nuts or Cardamom Powder

- Sprinkle chopped nuts or cardamom powder on top of the jaggery syrup for added flavor and texture.
