# Arepas Colombianas (Colombian Cornmeal Cakes)

Arepas are a staple in Colombian cuisine, often served as a side dish or used as a base for various fillings.

## Ingredients

### Dry Ingredients
- 2 cups **maíz harina** (cornmeal)
- 1/4 cup **harina de trigo** (wheat flour)

### Wet Ingredients
- 1/2 cup **leche evaporada** (evaporated milk)
- 1 large **huevo** (egg)
- 1/4 teaspoon **sal** (salt)
- 1 tablespoon **aceite vegetal** (vegetable oil)

### Additional Ingredients
- 1/4 cup **queso rallado** (shredded cheese, optional)
- Filling of your choice (e.g., chorizo, chicken, avocado)

## Arepas Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **aceite vegetal**
- 2 cloves **diente de ajo** (garlic), minced
- 1 tablespoon **leche evaporada**

---

### 1. Preparación del Maso
In a large mixing bowl, combine the cornmeal and wheat flour.

### 2. Mezcla de los Ingredientes Secos
Add the evaporated milk, egg, salt, and vegetable oil to the dry ingredients. Mix until the dough comes together.

### 3. Formado de las Arepas
Divide the dough into 4-6 equal pieces, depending on the desired size. Shape each piece into a ball and then flatten it slightly into a disk shape.

### 4. Cocinado en Comal o Sartén
Preheat a comal or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the arepas for about 4-5 minutes on each side, until they are golden brown and crispy.

### 5. Rebozado con Queso y Filling (Optional)
Once the arepas are cooked, sprinkle with shredded cheese if desired. Then, add your preferred filling.

---

## Optional: Arepa Rellena de Chorizo

- For a relleno of chorizo, cook sliced chorizo in a pan until browned and crispy. Add diced onions and bell peppers to the pan for added flavor.
- Assemble the arepas by placing a spoonful of the chorizo mixture on one half of the arepa and topping with the other half.
