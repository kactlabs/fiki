# Chicken Tandoori (Indian Marinated Chicken in Clay Oven)

Chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, grilled to perfection in a clay oven.

---

## Ingredients

### Category 1: Spices
- 2 tsp **garam masala**
- 1 tsp **cumin powder**
- 1 tsp **coriander powder**
- 1/2 tsp **cayenne pepper**

### Category 2: Yogurt Marinade
- 1 cup **plain yogurt**
- 2 tbsp **lemon juice**
- 1 tsp **ghee or vegetable oil**

### Category 3: Aromatics
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 inch **ginger**, grated

### Category 4: Chicken
- 1 1/2 lbs **boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs**, cut into 8 equal pieces

### Category 5: Baking Spices
- 1 tsp **cardamom powder**
- 1/2 tsp **cinnamon powder**

---

## Tandoori Masala Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tbsp **tandoori masala**
- 1 tbsp **plain yogurt**
- 1 tsp **lemon juice**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Yogurt Marinade
Combine yogurt, lemon juice, and ghee or oil in a bowl. Mix well.

### 2. Marinate the Chicken
Add garlic, ginger, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, and cayenne pepper to the yogurt marinade. Mix well. Add the chicken pieces and mix until they are well coated with the marinade. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

### 3. Prepare the Baking Spices
Mix cardamom powder and cinnamon powder in a small bowl.

### 4. Grill the Chicken
Preheat the clay oven to 400°F (200°C). Remove the chicken from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Thread the chicken pieces onto skewers, if using. Sprinkle the baking spices evenly over the chicken. Place the chicken in the clay oven and bake for 15-20 minutes or until cooked through.

### 5. Brush with Tandoori Masala Sauce (if applicable)
Remove the chicken from the clay oven and brush it with tandoori masala sauce.
