# Arroz con Mariscos y Chorizo (Spanish Seafood and Sausage Rice)

A hearty and flavorful Spanish-inspired rice dish loaded with a variety of seafood, chorizo, and vegetables.

## Ingredients

### Proteins and Seafood
- 1 pound **shrimp, peeled and deveined**
- 1/2 pound **mussels, scrubbed and debearded**
- 1/2 pound **clams, scrubbed**
- 6 ounces **chorizo, sliced**

### Vegetables
- 1 medium **onion, diced**
- 3 cloves **garlic, minced**
- 1 large **bell pepper, diced**
- 1 large **zucchini, sliced**

### Rice and Grains
- 2 cups **uncooked white rice**
- 4 cups **chicken broth, warmed**
- 2 tablespoons **olive oil**
- 1 teaspoon **smoked paprika**

### Spices and Seasonings
- 1 teaspoon **sausage seasoning**
- 1/2 teaspoon **pimentón**
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Arroz con Mariscos y Chorizo Sauce (if applicable)

- 2 tablespoons **olive oil**

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients
Prepare the seafood and chorizo by rinsing them under cold water and patting dry with paper towels. Slice the onion, bell pepper, and zucchini into desired sizes.

---

### Step 2: Cook the Chorizo
Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chorizo and cook until browned on both sides, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.

---

### Step 3: Sauté the Onion and Garlic
In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

---

### Step 4: Add the Bell Pepper and Zucchini
Add the diced bell pepper to the skillet and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Then add the sliced zucchini and continue cooking until both are tender, about 2-3 minutes.
