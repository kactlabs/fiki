# Chicken Fricassee (French)

A classic French dish of chicken cooked in a rich, creamy sauce made with butter, onions, and mushrooms.

---

## Ingredients

### Protein
- 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 8 pieces

### Vegetables
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 3 cups mixed mushrooms (button, cremini, shiitake), sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
- 2 celery stalks, sliced

### Pantry
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup dry white wine (such as Chardonnay or Sauvignon Blanc)
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour

### Cheese
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons grated Gruyère cheese
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened

---

## Chicken Fricassee Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **butter**
- 2 tablespoons **all-purpose flour**
- 1 cup **chicken broth**
- 1/4 cup **heavy cream**
- Salt and pepper to taste

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Preparation of Ingredients
Chop onion, mushrooms, carrots, celery, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper.

### 2. Searing Chicken Pieces
Heat butter in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken pieces and sear until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes per side. Remove from skillet and set aside.

---

### 3. Softening Onions and Mushrooms
Reduce heat to medium and add butter to skillet. Add chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their liquid and start to brown, about 5 minutes.

---

### 4. Adding Aromatics and Chicken Broth
Add garlic, carrots, celery, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper to skillet. Cook for 1 minute. Pour in white wine and chicken broth. Bring mixture to a simmer and cook until liquid has reduced by half, about 5 minutes.

---

### 5. Adding Chicken Pieces and Simmering
Return seared chicken pieces to skillet. Cover with lid and simmer over low
