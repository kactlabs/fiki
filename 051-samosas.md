# Samosas (Indian Fried or Baked Pastry Filled with Spiced Potatoes and Peas)

Samosas are a popular Indian snack that can be enjoyed as an appetizer or side dish.

---

## Ingredients

### Vegetable Oil
- 2 cups **Vegetable oil**
- 1 cup **All-purpose flour**

### Spice Mix
- 2 tablespoons **Garam masala powder**
- 1 tablespoon **Cumin powder**
- 1 tablespoon **Coriander powder**
- 1/2 teaspoon **Turmeric powder**
- 1/2 teaspoon **Red chili powder**
- Salt, to taste

### Filling Ingredients
- 2 large **Potatoes**, peeled and diced
- 1 cup **Fresh or frozen peas**
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves **Garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **Ghee** or vegetable oil
- Chopped cilantro, for garnish

### Pastry Dough Ingredients
- 2 cups **All-purpose flour**
- 1 teaspoon **Salt**
- 1/4 cup **Vegetable shortening**
- 1/4 cup **Lemon juice**

---

## Samosas Sauce (optional)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **Yogurt**
- 1 tablespoon **Ginger paste**
- 1 tablespoon **Garlic paste**
- Salt, to taste

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Spice Mix
Combine the garam masala powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt in a small bowl.

### 2. Boil the Potatoes and Peas
Boil the diced potatoes until they are tender. Drain the water and set aside. Steam the peas until they are tender. Set aside with the potatoes.

### 3. Make the Filling
Heat the ghee or oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and cooked potatoes and peas. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.

### 4. Prepare the Pastry Dough
Combine the flour, salt, shortening, and lemon juice in a bowl. Mix until the dough comes together.

### 5. Roll Out the Pastry Dough
Divide the pastry dough into small balls, about the size of a golf ball. Flatten each ball slightly into a disk shape. Roll out each disk into a thin circle
