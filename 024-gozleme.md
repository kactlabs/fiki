# **Gozleme** ([Turkish]

Thin, flaky pastry pockets filled with spiced cheese and herbs, often served as a snack or light meal.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat and Cheese
- 1 pound **ground beef**
- 1/2 cup **crumbled feta cheese**
- 1/4 cup **chopped fresh parsley**

### Spices and Herbs
- 1 teaspoon **paprika**
- 1/2 teaspoon **cumin**
- 1/2 teaspoon **salt**
- 1/4 teaspoon **black pepper**
- 1/4 teaspoon **cayenne pepper**

### Pastry Dough
- 2 cups **all-purpose flour**
- 1/2 cup **warm water**
- 1 tablespoon **olive oil**

---

## Gozleme Sauce (optional)

Mix the following:

- 1/2 cup **plain yogurt**
- 1 tablespoon **chopped fresh dill**
- 1 tablespoon **lemon juice**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Make the Pastry Dough
Combine flour, warm water, and olive oil in a large bowl. Mix until dough forms, then knead for 5 minutes.

---

### 2. Prepare the Filling
In a separate bowl, combine ground beef, crumbled feta cheese, chopped parsley, paprika, cumin, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper. Mix well.

---

### 3. Roll Out Pastry Dough
Divide dough into 4 equal pieces. Roll out each piece into a thin sheet, about 1/8 inch thick.

---

### 4. Assemble Gozleme
Place a tablespoon of filling in the center of each pastry sheet. Fold the pastry in half to form a triangle or rectangle shape, pressing edges together to seal.

---

### 5. Fry Gozleme
Heat about 2 inches of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. When oil is hot, add gozleme and fry until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side.

---

## Optional: **Spicy Red Pepper Variation**

Add 1-2 diced red peppers to the filling mixture for an extra kick of heat.
