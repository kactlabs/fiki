# Saffron Paella (Spanish Rice Dish with Seafood and Sausage)
Brief description of the dish: A classic Spanish rice dish from the Valencia region, made with saffron-infused rice, seafood, and spicy sausage.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 1 pound **shrimp**, peeled and deveined
- 8 ounces **chicken breast or thighs**, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 4 **sausages** (such as chorizo or morcilla), sliced

### Vegetables
- 2 cups **mixed seafood** (such as mussels, clams, and squid)
- 1 cup **artichoke hearts**
- 1 cup **roasted red bell peppers**, diced
- 1 large **onion**, diced
- 3 cloves **garlic**, minced

### Rice and Spices
- 2 cups **uncooked saffron-infused rice** (see note)
- 2 tablespoons **smoked paprika**
- 1 teaspoon **cumin**
- 1 teaspoon **paprika**
- Salt and pepper, to taste

### Sauces and Oils
- 2 tablespoons **olive oil**
- 2 cups **fish stock**
