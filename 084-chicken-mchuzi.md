# Chicken Mchuzi (Tanzanian Stewed Chicken)

Chicken Mchuzi is a popular Tanzanian dish made with chicken, potatoes, onions, and a blend of spices. The sauce is slightly thickened to create a rich and flavorful stew.

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces

### Vegetables
- 2 large onions, chopped
- 3-4 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 Scotch bonnet pepper, seeded and chopped
- 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro leaves

### Spices
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika powder
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

### Cooking oil
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

---

## Chicken Mchuzi Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 cup water
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Heat Oil in Pan
Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.

### 2. Sauté Onions
Sauté chopped onions until they are translucent and slightly caramelized.

### 3. Add Garlic and Scotch Bonnet Pepper
Add minced garlic and chopped Scotch bonnet pepper to the pan and sauté for another minute.

### 4. Add Chicken
Add chicken pieces to the pan and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.

### 5. Add Potatoes, Spices, and Cilantro
Add cubed potatoes, cumin, coriander, turmeric powder, paprika powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper to the pan. Stir well to combine.

### 6. Pour in Sauce
Pour in the chicken mchuzi sauce and bring the mixture to a boil.

### 7. Simmer Stew
Reduce heat to low and simmer the stew for about 20-25 minutes or until the potatoes are cooked through and the chicken is tender.
