# Dukkah (Egyptian Roasted Nut and Spice Mix)

Dukkah is a traditional Egyptian spice mix served as an appetizer or used to add flavor to various dishes.

## Ingredients

### Nuts and Seeds
- 1/2 cup **pumpkin seeds**
- 1/4 cup **sunflower seeds**

### Spices
- 3 tablespoons **ground coriander**
- 2 tablespoons **ground cumin**
- 2 tablespoons **cayenne pepper**
- 1 tablespoon **ground cinnamon**
- 1 tablespoon **ground cardamom**
- 1 teaspoon **ground cloves**
- 1/2 teaspoon **black pepper**

### Oils and Acids
- 2 tablespoons **olive oil**
- 1 tablespoon **white vinegar**

### Additional Ingredients
- 4-5 **dates**, pitted
- 2 **lemons**, zested

---

## Dukkah Sauce (optional)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **olive oil**
- 1 tablespoon **white vinegar**
- 1 teaspoon **honey**
- 1/2 teaspoon **ground cinnamon**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Roast the Nuts and Seeds
Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Spread pumpkin seeds and sunflower seeds on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, salt, and cayenne pepper. Roast for 10-12 minutes or until fragrant and lightly browned.

### 2. Grind the Nuts and Seeds
Allow the nuts and seeds to cool slightly. Transfer them to a spice grinder or food processor. Process until coarsely ground.

### 3. Mix with Spices
In a small bowl, combine ground coriander, cumin, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and black pepper.

### 4. Combine the Dukkah Mixture
Add the spice mixture to the ground nuts and seeds. Stir until well combined.

### 5. Add Dates and Lemon Zest
Stir in pitted dates and lemon zest.

---

## Optional: Stuffed Dates with Dukkah

- Stuff each date with a small amount of the dukkah mixture.
- Serve as an appetizer or use to add flavor to various dishes.
