# Beef Satay (Malaysian Grilled Meat Skewers)

Beef satay is a popular Malaysian dish made from marinated meat skewers, typically beef, chicken, or pork, grilled to perfection and served with spicy peanut sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Marination
- 500g **beef sirloin**, sliced into thin strips
- 2 tablespoons **soy sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **coconut milk**
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **grated ginger**
- 2 tablespoons **brown sugar**
- 1 tablespoon **tamarind paste**
- 1 teaspoon **paprika**

### Peanut Sauce
- 1/2 cup **peanut butter**
- 1/4 cup **coconut milk**
- 2 tablespoons **soy sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **lime juice**
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **grated ginger**
- 1/4 teaspoon **paprika**

### Garnishes
- 20 bamboo skewers, soaked in water for at least 30 minutes
- **Lime wedges**, for serving
- **Cucumber slices**, for serving

---

## Beef Satay Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **peanut butter**
- 1 tablespoon **coconut milk**
- 1 tablespoon **soy sauce**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare Marination
In a blender or food processor, combine soy sauce, coconut milk, garlic, ginger, brown sugar, tamarind paste, and paprika. Blend until smooth. Place the sliced beef in a large bowl and pour the marinade over it. Mix well to coat the beef evenly. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

### 2. Thread Skewers
Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Remove the beef from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Thread the beef onto the bamboo skewers, leaving a small space between each piece.

### 3. Grill Satay
Grill the satay for 2-3 minutes per side, or until cooked through and slightly charred.

### 4. Prepare Peanut Sauce
In a blender or food processor, combine peanut butter, coconut milk, soy sauce, lime juice, garlic, ginger, and paprika. Blend until smooth.
