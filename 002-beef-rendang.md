# Beef Rendang ([Malaysian/Indonesian Origin])

A rich and spicy beef stew originating from Malaysia and Indonesia, slow-cooked in coconut milk and aromatic spices.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 500g beef brisket or beef shin, cut into large pieces
- 250g beef fat (optional)

### Aromatics
- 2 onions, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 inches ginger, sliced

### Spices
- 2 tbsp coriander powder
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- 1 tsp turmeric powder
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper

### Coconut Milk and Liquid
- 500ml coconut milk
- 250ml beef broth or water
- 50g palm sugar (or brown sugar)

### Additional Ingredients
- 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised
- 2 kaffir lime leaves
- 1/4 cup tamarind paste

---

## Rendang Sauce

Mix the following:

- 250ml coconut milk
- 50g palm sugar (or brown sugar)
- 2 tbsp fish sauce (optional)

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Preparing the Spice Paste
Heat oil in a blender or food processor and sauté chopped onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant. Blend into a smooth paste.

---

### 2. Browning the Meat
In a large Dutch oven or heavy pot, heat oil over medium-high heat. Brown the beef pieces on all sides until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove from pot and set aside.

---

### 3. Adding Aromatics and Spices
Reduce heat to medium and add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger to the pot. Cook until onions are translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Cook for 1 minute.

---

### 4. Adding Coconut Milk and Liquid
Pour coconut milk, beef broth or water, palm sugar, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, tamarind paste into the pot. Stir well to combine.

---
