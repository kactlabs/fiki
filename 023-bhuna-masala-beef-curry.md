# Bhuna Masala Beef Curry (Indian)

A rich and flavorful North Indian beef curry made with tender beef, onions, garlic, ginger, and a blend of warming spices.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 500g **beef**, cut into small pieces
- 2 **onions**, thinly sliced

### Aromatics
- 3 **cloves** of garlic, minced
- 1 **inch** of fresh ginger, peeled and grated
- 2 **tablespoons** of coriander seeds
- 1 **teaspoon** of cumin seeds

### Spices
- 1 **teaspoon** ground cinnamon
- 1/2 **teaspoon** ground cardamom
- 1/2 **teaspoon** ground cayenne pepper
- 1 **teaspoon** garam masala powder
- 1/2 **teaspoon** turmeric powder
- Salt, to taste

### Oils and Acids
- 2 **tablespoons** vegetable oil
- 2 **tablespoons** tomato puree
- 2 **tablespoons** lemon juice

---

## Bhuna Masala Sauce

Mix the following:

- 4 **tablespoons** vegetable oil
- 2 **teaspoons** grated fresh ginger
- 1/2 **teaspoon** ground cumin
- 1/2 **teaspoon** garam masala powder
- 1/2 **teaspoon** turmeric powder

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Roasting the Spices
Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a small pan over medium heat. Add coriander seeds, cumin seeds, cinnamon stick, and cardamom pods. Dry roast until fragrant.

### 2. Grinding the Spices
Let the roasted spices cool. Grind them into a fine powder using a spice grinder or mortar and pestle.

### 3. Searing the Onions
Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add sliced onions and cook until they are golden brown, stirring occasionally.

### 4. Adding Aromatics and Spices
Add minced garlic, grated ginger, ground cumin, garam masala powder, turmeric powder, and roasted spice mixture to the pan. Cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly.

### 5. Adding Meat and Cooking
Add beef
