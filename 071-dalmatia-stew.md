# Dalmatia Stew (Croatian Hearty Meat and Vegetable Stew)

Dalmatia is a region in Croatia known for its rich culinary traditions, and this stew is a classic example of hearty Croatian cuisine.

## Ingredients

### Meat and Poultry
- 500g beef or lamb, cut into small pieces
- 250g pork, cut into small pieces

### Vegetables
- 2 large onions, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 medium carrots, peeled and sliced
- 2 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 1 large bell pepper, sliced
- 2 stalks celery, sliced

### Spices and Seasonings
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

### Broth and Acidity
- 500ml beef broth
- 250ml red wine
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp white vinegar

## Dalmatia Stew Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 100ml beef broth

---

### 1. Preparation of Meat and Vegetables
Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, carrots, potatoes, bell pepper, and celery. Cook until vegetables are tender.

---

### 2. Browning the Meat
Add beef or lamb pieces to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.

---

### 3. Adding Spices and Seasonings
Add paprika, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper to the pot and stir to combine.

---

### 4. Adding Broth and Acidity
Add beef broth, red wine, tomato paste, brown sugar, and white vinegar to the pot. Stir to combine.

---

### 5. Simmering the Stew
Bring stew to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for at least 2 hours or until meat is tender.

---

### Optional: Serving Suggestions

- Serve with crusty bread or over rice.
- Garnish with chopped fresh parsley or dill.
- Serve as part of a larger meal, such as with grilled meats or fish.
