# Shrikhand (Indian Sweet Yogurt Dessert)

Shrikhand is a popular Indian dessert made from sweetened yogurt, sugar, and cardamom. It is often served at special occasions and is a favorite among both children and adults.

---

## Ingredients

### Dry Ingredients
- 1 cup **granulated sugar**
- 1/2 teaspoon **ground cardamom**

### Wet Ingredients
- 1 cup **plain yogurt (dahi)**
- 1/4 cup **water**

### Optional
- 1 tablespoon **rose water** (optional)
- Chopped nuts or dried fruit for garnish (optional)

---

## Shrikhand Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **granulated sugar**
- 1 tablespoon **plain yogurt**
- 1/4 teaspoon **ground cardamom**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Sugar Syrup
Combine the granulated sugar, water, and ground cardamom in a saucepan. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar has dissolved.

### 2. Mix the Yogurt and Cardamom
In a separate bowl, whisk together the plain yogurt and ground cardamom.

### 3. Combine the Syrup and Yogurt Mixture
Gradually add the sugar syrup to the yogurt mixture, whisking constantly to avoid lumps.

### 4. Chill the Shrikhand
Refrigerate the shrikhand for at least 2 hours or overnight until chilled and set.

### 5. Serve (Optional)
Add a tablespoon of rose water if desired, along with chopped nuts or dried fruit for garnish, before serving.

---

## Optional: Variation Name

- To make a flavored shrikhand, add a few drops of food coloring to the mixture and stir well. You can also add other spices like cinnamon or saffron to give it an extra flavor boost.
