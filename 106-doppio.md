# Doppio (Italian Coffee)

Doppio is a traditional Italian shot of espresso served in a demitasse cup, typically enjoyed after meals or as a pick-me-up.

## Ingredients

### Beverages
- 1 **shot** of strong espresso

### Optional
- 0.5 **sugar**
- 0.5 **milk**

### Additional ingredients
- Demitasse cup (small espresso cup)

---

## Doppio Preparation

### 1. Prepare the Espresso Machine
Turn on the espresso machine and adjust the grind and tamping of the coffee beans to achieve a perfect shot.

---

### 2. Pull the Shot
Pull a single shot of espresso into the demitasse cup, aiming for about 1 ounce (30 ml) of liquid.

---

### 3. Add Sugar and Milk (Optional)
If desired, add sugar and/or milk to the espresso according to personal preference.

---

## Optional: Doppio Variations

- For a **Caffè Corretto**, add a small amount of liquor such as rum or grappa to the espresso.
- For a **Latte Doppio**, use steamed milk instead of water to create a creamy texture.
