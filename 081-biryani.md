# Biryani (South Asian Mixed Rice Dish)

A rich and flavorful rice dish originating from the Indian subcontinent, made with aromatic spices, basmati rice, and marinated meat or vegetables.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins/Meat
- 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs
- 1 cup lamb or beef, cut into small pieces

### Aromatics
- 2 medium onions, thinly sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)

### Spices
- 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
- 1 teaspoon cardamom powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon powder
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
- Salt, to taste

### Basmati Rice
- 2 cups basmati rice
- 3 cups water

### Vegetables
- 1 cup mixed vegetables, such as peas, carrots, and cauliflower
- 2 medium tomatoes, diced

### Flavor Enhancers
- 2 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro (optional)

---

## Biryani Sauce (optional)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons tomato puree
- 1 tablespoon yogurt
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
- Salt, to taste

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Marinade
Combine the marinade ingredients in a bowl and whisk until smooth. Add the chicken or lamb pieces and mix well to coat.

### 2. Cook the Basmati Rice
Rinse the basmati rice in a fine-mesh strainer until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside. In a medium saucepan, combine the rice and water. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked.
