# Arepa de Queso con Carne Mechada (Colombian Cheese and Shredded Beef Sandwich)

A traditional Colombian sandwich made with crispy arepas, melted cheese, and tender shredded beef.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 500g **beef shin or beef brisket**
- 250g **shredded chicken breast**

### Produce
- 2 **onions**
- 2 **garlic cloves**
- 1 **medium tomato**
- 2 **cucumbers**
- 1/4 cup **fresh cilantro leaves**

### Grains
- 500g **maize flour (arepa mix)**

### Dairy
- 250g **melted cheese (queso fresco or mozzarella)**

### Pantry
- 2 **tablespoons vegetable oil**
- 1 **teaspoon salt**
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder

---

Mix the following:

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Marinating the Beef
Combine beef shin, onions, garlic, tomato, cumin powder, salt, and black pepper in a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth. Transfer to a bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

### 2. Shredding the Chicken
Shred the chicken breast into thin strips. Season with salt and black pepper.

### 3. Cooking the Beef
Preheat a large skillet over medium heat. Remove the beef from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

### 4. Cooking the Shredded Chicken
Add vegetable oil to the same skillet used for the beef. Cook shredded chicken until heated through, about 3-4 minutes.

### 5. Assembling the Arepa
Divide maize flour into two equal parts. Gradually add warm water, kneading until a dough forms. Divide dough into four equal pieces and shape each piece into a ball. Flatten each ball slightly into an arepa shape using a rolling pin or your hands.
