# Khachapuri (Georgian Bread) ([Georgian/Description])

Traditional Georgian bread filled with melted cheese and egg.

---

## Ingredients

### Dough
- 1 cup **all-purpose flour**
- 1/2 teaspoon **salt**
- 1 tablespoon **sugar**
- 1 packet **active dry yeast**
- 1 cup **warm water**

### Filling
- 8 ounces **shredded mozzarella cheese**
- 2 large **eggs, beaten**

### Additional ingredients
- 1 tablespoon **olive oil**
- Fresh **dill, chopped (optional)**

---

## Khachapuri Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **butter, melted**
- 1 tablespoon **sour cream**
- 1 teaspoon **chopped fresh dill**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Activate Yeast
Combine warm water and yeast in a large mixing bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes until frothy.

---

### 2. Mix Dough Ingredients
In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, salt, sugar, and olive oil. Add the dry mixture to the yeast mixture and mix until a dough forms.

---

### 3. Knead Dough
Knead the dough for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place in a greased bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour.

---

### 4. Prepare Filling
Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). In a bowl, combine mozzarella cheese and beaten eggs.

---

### 5. Shape Dough
Punch down the dough and divide into 2 equal pieces. Roll out each piece into a long rectangle.

---

### 6. Assemble Khachapuri
Place one of the rectangles on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Spread half of the cheese mixture over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Repeat with the second rectangle and remaining cheese mixture.

---

### 7. Fold and Seal
Fold the edges of the khachapuri to form a crust. Use a knife or a pastry brush to seal the edges.

---

### 8. Brush with Egg Wash
Beat an egg in a small bowl and brush the egg wash over the khachapuri to give it a golden brown color.
