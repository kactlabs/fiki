# Jollof Rice with Grilled Sausage and Peppers ([West African])

A classic one-pot dish originating from the West African region, often debated as a national dish of Ghana or Nigeria. This version combines spicy sausages and sweet bell peppers for added flavor.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 1 pound **sausages (any variety)**
- 1 pound **chicken breast or thighs**

### Grains
- 2 cups **long-grain rice**

### Vegetables
- 4 **bell peppers**, sliced
- 2 **onions**, chopped
- 3 **garlic cloves**, minced

### Spices and Seasonings
- 2 teaspoons **ground cumin**
- 1 teaspoon **paprika**
- 1 teaspoon **salt**
- 1/2 teaspoon **black pepper**
- 2 tablespoons **fish sauce (optional)**
- 1 tablespoon **hot sauce (such as sriracha)**

### Oils
- 3 tablespoons **vegetable oil**

---

## Jollof Rice Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 cups **chicken or vegetable broth**
- 1 cup **water**
- 2 tablespoons **tomato paste**
- 1 tablespoon **brown sugar**
- 1 teaspoon **ground cumin**
- 1/2 teaspoon **paprika**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Sausages and Peppers
Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium heat. Grill the sausages until browned, then set aside. Grill the bell peppers for about 5 minutes on each side until slightly charred.

---

### 2. Cook the Onions and Garlic
In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and cook until they are translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute, until fragrant.

---

### 3. Cook the Rice
Add the remaining 2 tablespoons of oil to the pot with the onions and garlic. Stir in the cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Add the rice and stir to coat the grains evenly with spices. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the rice is lightly toasted.
