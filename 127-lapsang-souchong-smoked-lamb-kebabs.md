# Lapsang Souchong Smoked Lamb Kebabs (Chinese)

Lapsang Souchong smoked lamb kebabs are a popular Chinese dish that combines the smoky flavor of Lapsang Souchong tea with tender lamb. The result is a delicious and aromatic meal that's perfect for special occasions.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 500g boneless lamb shoulder, cut into 2cm cubes

### Marinade
- 120ml soy sauce
- 60ml Shaoxing wine
- 30g Lapsang Souchong tea leaves
- 20g brown sugar
- 10g cornstarch
- 5g five-spice powder
- 3g black pepper

### Aromatics
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
- 1 scallion, chopped (optional)

### Sauce
- 60ml soy sauce
- 30ml Shaoxing wine
- 20g hoisin sauce
- 10g rice vinegar
- 5g cornstarch
