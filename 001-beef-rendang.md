# Beef Rendang ([Malaysian Cuisine])

A rich and flavorful beef stew originating from Malaysia, typically served with steamed rice or roti.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat Category
- 500g **beef brisket or chuck**, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2 tablespoons **vegetable oil**

### Spice Blend Category
- 4 **cinnamon sticks**
- 3 **cardamom pods**
- 1 **star anise**
- 6-8 **whole cloves**
- 1 **teaspoon ground cumin**
- 1 **teaspoon ground coriander**
- 1/2 teaspoon **turmeric powder**
- 1/2 teaspoon **cayenne pepper (optional)**
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

### Aromatics Category
- 4 **shallots**, chopped
- 2 **garlic cloves**, minced
- 2 **galangal** or **ginger** pieces, peeled and chopped

### Liquid Category
- 500ml **coconut milk**
- 250ml **beef broth**
- 2 tablespoons **tamarind paste**

---

## Beef Rendang Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 4 tablespoons **vegetable oil**
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **grated galangal** or **ginger**
- 2 tablespoons **tamarind paste**
- 2 tablespoons **coconut milk**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Marinade the Meat
In a large bowl, combine beef pieces, vegetable oil, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and cayenne pepper (if using). Mix well to coat the meat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

---

### 2. Sauté Aromatics
Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add shallots, garlic, and galangal or ginger. Cook until the mixture is fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

---

### 3. Brown the Meat
Remove the meat from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. In the same pan, add remaining oil if necessary, then brown the beef pieces in batches until they are nicely browned on all sides, about 5 minutes per batch.
