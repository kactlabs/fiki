# Dish Name (Origin)
Turkish Doner Kebab is a popular Turkish dish made from layers of lamb or beef stacked on a vertical spit and served in a crispy sesame-topped bun with salad, vegetables, and sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Meats
- 500g **lamb or beef**, thinly sliced
- 2 **onions**, finely chopped
- 3 **cloves** **garlic**, minced
- 1 **teaspoon** **salt**
- 1/2 **teaspoon** **black pepper**

### Spices
- 1 **teaspoon** **paprika**
- 1 **teaspoon** **cumin**
- 1/2 **teaspoon** **coriander**

### Sauces
- 2 **tablespoons** **yogurt**
- 1 **tablespoon** **lemon juice**
- 1/4 **cup** **olive oil**

### Vegetables
- 1 **large tomato**, sliced
- 1/4 **cup** **chopped fresh parsley**
- 1/4 **cup** **chopped fresh mint**
- 2 **cucumber slices**
- 1 **carrot stick**

### 1. Prepare the Meat
Preheat a large skillet or pan over medium heat. In a small bowl, mix together salt, black pepper, paprika, cumin, and coriander. Add the sliced meat and cook for about 5 minutes per side, until it's well browned.
