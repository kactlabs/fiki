# Kibbeh Nabulsy (Lebanese Meatball Stuffed Grape Leaves)

Kibbeh nabulsy is a traditional Lebanese dish made by stuffing grape leaves with a mixture of ground meat, bulgur, and spices. The grape leaves are then rolled and cooked in a flavorful broth.

## Ingredients

### Meats
- 1 pound **ground lamb**
- 1/2 pound **ground beef**

### Grains
- 1 cup **bulgur**
- 1/4 cup **all-purpose flour**

### Spices and Seasonings
- 2 teaspoons **salt**
- 1 teaspoon **black pepper**
- 1 teaspoon **paprika**
- 1/2 teaspoon **cumin**

### Filling Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons **olive oil**
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 2 tablespoons **all-purpose flour**

### Grape Leaves
- 20-24 **fresh grape leaves**

## Kibbeh Nabulsy Sauce (optional)

Mix the following:

- 2 cups **water**
- 2 tablespoons **olive oil**
- 1 tablespoon **lemon juice**
- 1 teaspoon **sumac**

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare Grape Leaves
Soak grape leaves in water for at least 30 minutes to make them pliable.

### 2. Make Filling
Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute. Stir in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes. Mix in ground lamb, ground beef, salt, black pepper, paprika, and cumin.

### 3. Cook Filling
Add bulgur to the pan with the meat mixture and stir well. Cook over medium heat until the mixture is well combined.

### 4. Prepare Grape Leaves for Stuffing
Remove grape leaves from water and pat dry.

### 5. Assemble Kibbeh Nabulsy
Lay a grape leaf flat on a work surface, with the stem end facing you. Place about 1 tablespoon of filling in the center of the leaf. Fold the stem end over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll the leaf into a neat package. Repeat with remaining leaves and filling.
