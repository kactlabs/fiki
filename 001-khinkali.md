# Khinkali ([Georgian Beef Dumplings])

Khinkali are traditional Georgian dumplings filled with spiced meat and herbs, served with a tangy lemon-garlic sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat Filling
- 500g **ground beef**
- 250g **finely chopped pork**
- 1 large **onion**, finely chopped
- 2 cloves of **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **chopped fresh parsley**
- 1 teaspoon **dried dill weed**
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

### Dough
- 2 cups **all-purpose flour**
- 2 teaspoons **salt**
- 1/4 cup **warm water**

### Lemon-Garlic Sauce
- 1 cup **freshly squeezed lemon juice**
- 3 cloves of **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **olive oil**
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

---

## Khinkali Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **olive oil**
- 2 tablespoons **lemon juice**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Make the Dough
Combine flour, salt, and warm water in a bowl. Mix until dough forms. Knead for 5 minutes.

### 2. Prepare the Meat Filling
In a pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook until softened. Add garlic, parsley, dill weed, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2 minutes. Add ground beef and pork; mix well. Cook until meat is browned.

### 3. Assemble the Dumplings
Divide dough into small balls, about the size of a small egg. Roll each ball into a thin circle. Place a tablespoon of meat filling in the center of each circle. Fold dough over to form a half-moon shape and press edges together to seal.

### 4. Boil the Dumplings
In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Carefully add khinkali; cook for 10 minutes.

### 5. Serve with Sauce
Drain dumplings and serve hot with lemon-garlic sauce spooned over the top.

---

## Optional: Pan-Fried Khinkali

- Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add khinkali; cook until golden brown on both sides.
- Serve hot with lemon-garlic sauce.
