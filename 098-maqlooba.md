#  Maqlooba (Jordanian Stuffed Rice Pilaf)

Maqlooba is a traditional Jordanian dish that combines flavorful rice pilaf with tender lamb or chicken, and aromatic spices.

## Ingredients

### Meat/Protein Category
- 1 pound boneless lamb shoulder, cut into small pieces
- 1 pound boneless chicken breast or thighs, cut into small pieces

### Rice Category
- 2 cups long-grain white rice
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 4 cups water

### Aromatic Spices and Herbs Category
- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- Salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint

### Vegetables Category
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 medium tomatoes, diced
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

## Maqlooba Sauce (optional)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1/4 cup water
- Salt and pepper, to taste
