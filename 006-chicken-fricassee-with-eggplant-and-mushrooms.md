# Chicken Fricassee with Eggplant and Mushrooms (French)

A classic French dish from the south of France, made with chicken, eggplant, and mushrooms in a rich and flavorful sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 cup mixed mushrooms (button, cremini, shiitake), sliced

### Vegetables
- 2 large eggplants, sliced into 1/4-inch thick rounds
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 carrots, peeled and grated
- 2 celery stalks, chopped

### Aromatics
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika
- Salt and pepper, to taste

### Liquid
- 1 cup dry white wine
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1/2 cup heavy cream

---

## Chicken Fricassee Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 cup dry white wine
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1/2 cup heavy cream

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Searing the Chicken and Vegetables
Heat the butter in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown, about 3-4 minutes. Add the onions, garlic, carrots, and celery, and cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.

---

### 2. Browing the Eggplant
Add the sliced eggplant to the pot and cook until it starts to brown on all sides, about 5 minutes per side. Remove the eggplant from the pot and set aside with the chicken.

---

### 3. Making the Sauce
Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables in the pot and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly. Add the white wine, chicken broth, and heavy cream to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer until the sauce has thickened, about 5-7 minutes.
