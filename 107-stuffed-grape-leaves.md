# Stuffed Grape Leaves (Dolmas) (Turkish Appetizer)

A traditional Turkish dish made by filling grape leaves with a mix of rice, meat, and spices, then rolling them up and serving as an appetizer.

## Ingredients

### Meat Filling
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1/2 cup chopped onion
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika

### Rice Filling
- 1 cup cooked white rice
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt to taste

### Grape Leaves
- 20-24 grape leaves (fresh or jarred)

### Assembly and Cooking
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Water for boiling

### Sauce (optional)
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Salt to taste

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Meat Filling
Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the ground beef, salt, black pepper, and paprika. Cook, breaking up the meat with a spoon, until the mixture is browned and cooked through.

### 2. Prepare the Rice Filling
Heat the olive oil in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add the cooked rice and cook, stirring constantly, until lightly toasted and fragrant.

### 3. Prepare the Grape Leaves
If using fresh grape leaves, blanch them in boiling water for 30 seconds to make them pliable. If using jarred grape leaves, rinse them with cold water and drain well.
