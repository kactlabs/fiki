# Vietnamese Banh Bot Loc (Vietnamese Miniature Dumplings)

Banh Bot Loc is a popular Vietnamese snack made from small, chewy dumplings filled with shrimp or pork. The dish is typically served in a sweet broth.

---

## Ingredients

### Noodles
- 1 cup **glass noodles**

### Filling
- 1/2 pound **shrimp, peeled and deveined**
- 1/4 cup **pork, finely chopped**
- 1 tablespoon **fish sauce**
- 1 tablespoon **sugar**
- 1 teaspoon **salt**
- 1 egg, beaten

### Broth
- 2 cups **chicken broth**
- 1 cup **water**

### Garnish
- 1/4 cup **green onions, thinly sliced**
- 1/4 cup **bean sprouts**
- 1/4 cup **pickled ginger, thinly sliced**

---

## Banh Bot Loc Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **fish sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **sugar**
- 1 tablespoon **chicken broth**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Filling
Combine shrimp, pork, fish sauce, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Mix well.

### 2. Cook the Glass Noodles
Cook glass noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

### 3. Assemble the Dumplings
Combine cooked noodles, filling mixture, and beaten egg in a bowl. Mix until well combined.

### 4. Steam the Dumplings
Steam dumplings for 5-7 minutes or until they float to the surface.

### 5. Prepare the Broth
Combine chicken broth, water, fish sauce, sugar, and salt in a pot. Bring to a boil then reduce heat and simmer.

### 6. Serve the Banh Bot Loc
Add cooked dumplings to the hot broth. Garnish with green onions, bean sprouts, and pickled ginger.

---

## Optional: Spicy Banh Bot Loc

- Add 1/4 teaspoon **red pepper flakes** to the filling mixture for an extra spicy kick.

---

## Note:
Banh Bot Loc can be made ahead of time by preparing the filling and noodles. Store in the refrigerator overnight then assemble and steam the next day.
