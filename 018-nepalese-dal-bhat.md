# Nepalese Dal Bhat (Lentil Soup with Steamed Rice)

Nepalese Dal Bhat is a staple dish in Himalayan cuisine, consisting of a flavorful lentil soup made with split red lentils and steamed rice.

---

## Ingredients

### Protein Category
- 1 cup **split red lentils**
- 2 cups **chicken or vegetable broth**

### Vegetable Category
- 2 medium **onions**, chopped
- 3 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 teaspoon **ground cumin**
- 1 teaspoon **ground coriander**
- 1/4 teaspoon **cayenne pepper**
- Salt and pepper, to taste

### Rice Category
- 2 cups **uncooked long-grain rice**

### Additional Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons **vegetable oil**
- Fresh cilantro, for garnish

---

## Dal Bhat Sauce (optional)

Mix the following:

- 1 tablespoon **ghee** or vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon **ground cumin**
- 1 teaspoon **ground coriander**
- Salt and pepper, to taste
