# Jamaican Jerk Chicken Wrap (Jamaican)

A flavorful dish inspired by the Caribbean, featuring jerk chicken in a wrap.

---

## Ingredients

### Protein
- 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts

### Spices and Seasonings
- 2 tablespoons allspice
- 2 tablespoons thyme
- 2 tablespoons scotch bonnet peppers (optional)
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

### Wrap and Filling
- 4 large flour tortillas
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 1 cup diced tomatoes
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons jerk seasoning (store-bought or homemade)
- 1/4 cup melted butter

### Additional ingredients (optional)
- Grilled pineapple slices
- Coconut rice
- Fried plantains

---

## Jamaican Jerk Chicken Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons jerk seasoning (store-bought or homemade)
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- 1 tablespoon honey

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Marinating the Chicken
Combine all spice and seasoning ingredients in a bowl. Add chicken breasts and mix until coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

### 2. Preparing the Jerk Seasoning Mixture
In a blender or food processor, combine scotch bonnet peppers (if using), brown sugar, thyme, allspice, salt, and black pepper. Blend until smooth.

### 3. Grilling the Chicken
Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Remove chicken from marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Grill for 5-6 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

### 4. Assembling the Wrap
Warm tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds. Spread jerk seasoning mixture on each tortilla, leaving a 1-inch border around edges. Add shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded carrots, chopped cilantro, grilled chicken, and melted butter.

### 5. Optional: Adding Additional Ingredients
Top with grilled pineapple slices, coconut rice, or fried plantains for added flavor and texture.
