# Thai Massaman Curry with Chicken and Pineapple ([Thailand/Hearty Beef-Free Curry])

Massaman curry is a rich, flavorful Thai dish originating from the southern region of Thailand. This variation features chicken and pineapple as main ingredients, making it a great option for those looking for a beef-free version.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 cup firm pineapple chunks

### Aromatics
- 2 medium onions, thinly sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
- 2 tablespoons fish sauce (optional)

### Spices and seasonings
- 2 tablespoons Massaman curry paste
- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

### Oils and fats
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons coconut cream

### Additional ingredients
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, carrots, and green beans), sliced
- 1 cup cooked jasmine rice
- Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
- Lime wedges for serving

---

## Massaman Curry Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons Massaman curry paste
- 2 cups coconut milk
- 2 cups water
- 2 tablespoons fish sauce (optional)
- 1 tablespoon palm sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the spices
In a small bowl, mix together cumin, coriander, turmeric powder, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and black pepper.

### 2. Cook the onions
Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and cook until they are lightly browned and caramelized, stirring occasionally.

### 3. Add aromatics and spices
Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the saucepan with the cooked onions. Cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, until fragrant. Stir in the prepared spice mixture and cook for an additional minute
