# Sosatie de Kudu ([South African Description])

Traditional South African meat skewers, typically made with kudu or ostrich, are a hearty and flavorful dish.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 500g **kudu steak**, cut into 2.5cm cubes
- 250g **ostrich liver**, sliced
- 1/4 cup **white wine vinegar**
- 1/4 cup **olive oil**

### Spice Blend
- 2 tablespoons **curry powder**
- 1 tablespoon **paprika**
- 1 teaspoon **salt**
- 1/2 teaspoon **black pepper**
- 1/2 teaspoon **cumin seeds**

### Aromatics
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 onion, sliced

---

## Sosatie de Kudu Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **white wine vinegar**
- 1 tablespoon **olive oil**
- 1 teaspoon **Dijon mustard**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Preparing the Spice Blend
Combine curry powder, paprika, salt, black pepper, and cumin seeds in a bowl.

### 2. Marinating the Kudu Steak
In a large bowl, combine kudu steak, white wine vinegar, olive oil, garlic, onion, and spice blend. Mix well to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

### 3. Preparing the Ostrich Liver
Slice ostrich liver into thin strips and marinate in separate bowl with remaining marinade ingredients (excluding kudu steak) for 2 hours.

### 4. Skewering the Meat
Thread alternate pieces of kudu steak, ostrich liver, and onion onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece.

---

## Optional: Braai-Style Sosatie de Kudu

- Grill sosatie de kudu over medium-high heat for 8-10 minutes per side, or until cooked to desired level of doneness.
