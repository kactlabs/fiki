# Feijoada (Brazilian Bean Stew)

Feijoada is a hearty Brazilian stew made from black beans and various meats, typically served on weekends or special occasions.

## Ingredients

### Meat Category
- 1 pound **pork ribs**
- 1 pound **beef brisket**

### Vegetable Category
- 2 large **onions**, chopped
- 3 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 2 medium **potatoes**, peeled and cubed
- 1 large **carrot**, peeled and sliced
- 1 large **celery stalk**, sliced

### Spices Category
- 1 teaspoon **ground cumin**
- 1 teaspoon **dried oregano**
- 1/2 teaspoon **salt**
- 1/4 teaspoon **black pepper**

### Bean Category
- 1 pound dried black beans, soaked overnight and drained

### Additional Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons **vegetable oil**
- 2 cups **beef broth**
- 1 cup **red wine vinegar**

## Feijoada Sauce (Malagueta Pepper Sauce)

Mix the following:

- 4 **malagueta peppers**, seeded and chopped
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1/4 cup **red wine vinegar**
- 1 tablespoon **brown sugar**
- 1 teaspoon **salt**

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Preparing the Meat
Heat oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add pork ribs and cook until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove from pot and set aside.

### 2. Browning the Beef
Add beef brisket to the pot and brown on all sides, about 5 minutes. Remove from pot and set aside with pork ribs.

### 3. Soaking the Beans
In a large bowl, combine soaked black beans, water, cumin, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.

### 4. Sauteing the Onions and Garlic
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onions and garlic; cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

### 5. Adding the Potatoes and Carrots
Add potatoes and carrots to the skillet with onion mixture. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

### 6. Assembling the Stew
Add browned pork ribs and beef brisket back into the pot. Add sautéed onions, garlic, potatoes, and carrots; stir to combine.
