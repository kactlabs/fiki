# Miso Glazed Salmon with Sesame Grits (Japanese Comfort Food)

Miso glazed salmon is a popular Japanese dish that combines the savory flavors of miso paste with the richness of brown sugar and soy sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Pantry
- 1/2 cup **white miso paste**
- 1/4 cup **brown sugar**
- 2 tablespoons **soy sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **rice vinegar**

### Proteins
- 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)

### Grains
- 1 cup **sesame seeds**
- 1 cup **coconut milk**
- 1/2 cup **water**

### Produce
- 2 tablespoons **grated fresh ginger**
- 1 tablespoon **chopped scallions**
- 1 tablespoon **toasted sesame oil**
- Salt and pepper, to taste

---

## Miso Glaze Sauce

Mix the following:

- 1/4 cup **white miso paste**
- 2 tablespoons **brown sugar**
- 2 tablespoons **soy sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **rice vinegar**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Preheat Oven
Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).

### 2. Make Sesame Grits
In a medium saucepan, combine sesame seeds, coconut milk, and water. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 5 minutes or until the mixture has thickened slightly.

### 3. Prepare Miso Glaze Sauce
In a small bowl, whisk together miso paste, brown sugar, soy sauce, and rice vinegar until smooth.

### 4. Season Salmon Fillets
Season salmon fillets with salt, pepper, and grated ginger.

### 5. Bake Salmon Fillets
Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Drizzle with toasted sesame oil. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until cooked through.

### 6. Glaze with Miso Sauce
Brush the miso glaze sauce over the baked salmon fillets during the last 2 minutes of cooking.

### 7. Serve with Sesame Grits
Serve the glazed salmon with sesame grits, garnished with chopped scallions and toasted sesame seeds.
