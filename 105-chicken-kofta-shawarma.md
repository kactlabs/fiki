# Chicken Kofta Shawarma (Turkish Street Food)

Chicken kofta shawarma is a popular Turkish street food that consists of juicy chicken skewers, served in a crispy pita bread with vegetables, tahini sauce, and a drizzle of sumac.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 500g **boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs**, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 250g **lamb mince**

### Spices
- 2 tsp **ground cumin**
- 1 tsp **paprika**
- 1 tsp **cayenne pepper**
- 1 tsp **salt**
- 1/2 tsp **black pepper**
- 1/4 tsp **cinnamon**
- 1/4 tsp **allspice**

### Vegetables
- 1 large **onion**, thinly sliced
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 large **bell pepper**, sliced

### Tahini Sauce
- 100g **tahini**
- 50g **lemon juice**
- 20g **olive oil**
- 10g **minced garlic**
- Salt and water to taste

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Marinate the Chicken
In a large bowl, combine chicken, lamb mince, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, black pepper, cinnamon, and allspice. Mix well.

### 2. Grill the Koftas
Thread chicken and lamb mixture onto skewers, leaving a small space between each piece. Brush with olive oil and grill over medium heat for 10-12 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

### 3. Prepare the Vegetables
In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add bell pepper and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

### 4. Assemble the Shawarma
Warm pita bread by wrapping it in foil and heating in the oven for 5 minutes. Slice grilled koftas into thin strips. Add a few slices of kofta to each pita, followed by vegetables, tahini sauce, and sumac-tah
