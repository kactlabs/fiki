# Japanese Teriyaki Chicken Buns (Japanese-style BBQ Chicken Buns)

Brief description of the dish: A popular Japanese-inspired dish consisting of juicy chicken breast marinated in a sweet teriyaki sauce, served in a soft and fluffy bun with crunchy slaw and pickled carrots.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 500g boneless, skinless chicken breast

### Marinade
- 200ml soy sauce
- 100ml sake or mirin
- 50g brown sugar
- 20g grated ginger
- 2 cloves garlic, minced

### Bun and Slaw
- 1 package of soft and fluffy Japanese-style milk bread buns (about 12-15 buns)
- 200g shredded napa cabbage
- 100g carrots, peeled and thinly sliced

---

## Teriyaki Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 150ml soy sauce
- 50g brown sugar
- 20g grated ginger
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 20g sake or mirin

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Marinate Chicken Breast
Combine marinade ingredients in a bowl and whisk until smooth. Add chicken breast and marinate for at least 30 minutes or overnight.

### 2. Grill Chicken Breast
Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Remove chicken from marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Grill chicken for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

---

### 3. Prepare Slaw and Carrots
In a small bowl, combine shredded cabbage and carrots. In another bowl, whisk together soy sauce, sugar, grated ginger, and garlic to make dressing. Pour dressing over slaw mixture and toss until combined.

### 4. Assemble Buns
Cut milk bread buns in half lengthwise and toast until lightly browned. Spread a layer of mayonnaise on the bottom half of each bun. Add sliced grilled chicken breast, followed by a spoonful of slaw mixture, pickled carrots, and finally the top half of the bun.

---

## Optional: Korean-Style Gochujang Mayo

Mix 1/4 cup gochujang with 2 tablespoons mayonnaise for an extra spicy kick!
