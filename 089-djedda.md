# Djedda (Sudanese Stewed Meat and Vegetables)

Djedda is a hearty stew originating from Sudan, made with a mixture of meat, vegetables, and spices.

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 1 lb **beef or lamb**, cut into small pieces
- 1/2 cup **pork or beef broth**

### Vegetables
- 2 medium **onions**
- 3 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 2 medium **carrots**
- 2 stalks **celery**
- 1 large **potato**, peeled and diced

### Spices
- 2 tsp **ground cumin**
- 1 tsp **ground coriander**
- 1 tsp **cayenne pepper**
- 1/2 tsp **salt**
- 1/4 tsp **black pepper**

### Condiments
- 2 tbsp **all-purpose flour**
- 2 tbsp **vegetable oil**
- 2 tbsp **vinegar**
- 1 tsp **ground ginger**

## Djedda Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 tbsp **tomato paste**
- 2 tbsp **olive oil**
- 2 cloves **minced garlic**

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Preparing the Meat
Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the meat and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.

### 2. Adding Vegetables and Spices
Add the onions, carrots, celery, cumin, coriander, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes.

### 3. Mixing in the Meat Broth and Ginger
Mix the broth and ginger into the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes.

### 4. Adding the Potato and Tomatoes
Add the potato to the pot and continue to simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.

### 5. Mixing in the Flour and Vinegar
Mix the flour and vinegar into the pot. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.

## Optional: Variation with Peanuts

- Add 1/4 cup **peanut paste** to the sauce during step 3.
- Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley or cilantro.

---

Note: Traditionally, Djedda is made in a clay pot over an open flame, but this recipe uses a stovetop.
