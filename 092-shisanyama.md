# Shisanyama (South African Braai)

Brief description of the dish: Shisanyama is a traditional South African braai, where various meats are grilled over an open flame. This recipe focuses on a classic combination of boerewors, steak, and vegetables.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 500g **boerewors sausage**
- 1 **steak (300g)**

### Vegetables
- 2 **bell peppers (any color)**
- 2 **zucchinis**
- 1 **large onion**

### Marinades and Sauces
- 150ml **braai sauce (see below for recipe)**
- 2 **tablespoons olive oil**

---

## Braai Sauce

Mix the following:

- 150ml **ketchup**
- 50g **brown sugar**
- 2 **teaspoons Worcestershire sauce**
- 1 **teaspoon dried oregano**
- Salt and pepper to taste
