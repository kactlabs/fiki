# [Gyro Wrap] (Greek/Mediterranean Street Food)

A traditional Greek gyro wrap filled with juicy lamb, crispy vegetables, and tangy tzatziki sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat and Poultry
- 1 pound **lamb**, thinly sliced
- 4 tablespoons **olive oil**
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **dried oregano**
- 1 teaspoon **ground cumin**

### Vegetables
- 1 large **red onion**, thinly sliced
- 2 large **bell peppers** (any color), seeded and thinly sliced
- 2 large **tomatoes**, diced

### Dairy and Condiments
- 1 cup **yogurt**
- 1/4 cup **dill**, chopped
- 2 tablespoons **lemon juice**
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Pita Bread and Toppings
- 4 **pita breads**
- Chopped fresh **parsley** or **dill**
- Thinly sliced **red onion**

---

## Gyro Wrap Sauce (Tzatziki)

Mix the following:

- 1 cup **yogurt**
- 1/2 cup **cucumber**, peeled and grated
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **lemon juice**
- Salt and pepper to taste

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Lamb
Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, garlic, oregano, and cumin. Brush the mixture evenly onto both sides of the lamb slices.

Grill the lamb for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. Transfer the cooked lamb to a plate and let it rest for a few minutes.

### 2. Prepare the Vegetables
In a separate pan, heat a couple of tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the sliced red onion and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until caramelized and tender.

Add the bell peppers to the pan and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes per side, or until tender but still crisp. Finally, add the diced tomatoes and cook for another minute.
