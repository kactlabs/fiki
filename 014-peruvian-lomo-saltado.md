# Peruvian Lomo Saltado ([Peruvian Stir-Fried Beef])

A classic Peruvian dish that combines stir-fried beef, onions, tomatoes, and French fries, served with steamed rice.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 1 pound **sirloin steak**, sliced into thin strips
- 2 eggs, beaten (for egg omelette)

### Vegetables
- 2 large **onions**, sliced
- 2 large **tomatoes**, diced
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 cup **French fries**
- 1 cup **scallions** (green onions), chopped

### Sauce
- 1/4 cup **soy sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **Peruvian aji amarillo pepper paste**

### Additional ingredients
- 2 tablespoons **vegetable oil**
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- 2 tablespoons **chimichurri sauce** (see below)

---

## Chimichurri Sauce

Mix the following:

- 1/4 cup **parsley**, chopped
- 1/4 cup **oregano**, chopped
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 2 tablespoons **red wine vinegar**
- 1 tablespoon **olive oil**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the ingredients
Slice the onions, tomatoes, and scallions. Cut the French fries into bite-sized pieces.

### 2. Cook the French fries
Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the French fries and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes. Drain on paper towels.

### 3. Stir-fry the beef and onions
In a separate large skillet or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over high heat. Add the sliced beef and cook until browned, about 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.

### 4. Cook the garlic and tomatoes
In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until they start to soften, about 2-3 minutes.
