# Chicken Shawarma Wrap ([Middle Eastern/Street Food])

A flavorful street food wrap from the Middle East, filled with juicy chicken, crunchy veggies, and creamy tahini sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat Category
- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast **cut into thin strips**
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 2 cloves garlic, minced

### Veggie Category
- 1 large red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 large cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
- 1 large tomato, diced
- 4 lettuce leaves
- 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tbsp pickled turnips (optional)

### Tahini Sauce Category
- 1/2 cup tahini paste
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp salt

---

## Chicken Shawarma Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 tbsp pomegranate molasses
- 1 tbsp sumac
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp black pepper

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Marinate the Chicken
In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, black pepper, and garlic. Add the chicken strips and mix well to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

---

### 2. Grill the Chicken
Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off. Cook the chicken for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Transfer to a plate and cover with foil to keep warm.

---

### 3. Prepare the Veggies
In a separate bowl, combine sliced bell pepper, cucumber, tomato, lettuce leaves, parsley, and pickled turnips (if using).

---

### 4. Assemble the Shawarma Wrap
