# Turkish Doner Kebab (Doner Kebap) (Turkish Meat Pies)

A popular Turkish dish made with layers of juicy meat stacked on a vertical spit, served in a warm pita bread with vegetables and sauce.

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 1 kg **lamb or beef** (or a combination of both)
- 2 tablespoons **olive oil**
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **salt**
- 1 teaspoon **black pepper**
- 1 teaspoon **cumin**
- 1 teaspoon **paprika**

### Vegetables
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 bell peppers (any color), sliced
- 2 tomatoes, diced

### Pita Bread and Sauce
- 4 **pita breads**
- 1/4 cup **toum** (garlic sauce) or **yogurt-based sauce**

## Turkish Doner Kebab Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **olive oil**
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **lemon juice**
- 1 teaspoon **salt**
- 1/2 teaspoon **black pepper**

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Meat
Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, garlic, salt, black pepper, cumin, and paprika. Rub the mixture all over the lamb or beef, making sure to coat it evenly.

### 2. Cook the Meat
Cook the meat for about 10-15 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it thinly against the grain.

### 3. Prepare the Vegetables
Heat some oil in a pan and sauté the chopped onion, sliced bell peppers, and diced tomatoes until they are tender and lightly browned.

### 4. Assemble the Doner Kebab
Slice the cooked meat into thin strips. Place a few slices of meat onto each pita bread, followed by some sautéed vegetables. Drizzle with toum or yogurt-based sauce and serve immediately.

### 5. Serve
Serve the Turkish Doner Kebab hot, garnished with fresh parsley or cilantro if desired.

Optional: Add some pickled turnips or cucumbers on top of the meat for extra flavor and texture.
