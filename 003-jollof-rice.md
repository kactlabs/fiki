# Jollof Rice ([West African Description])

A classic one-pot dish from West Africa, Jollof Rice is a flavorful and spicy rice recipe that's perfect for special occasions.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins
- 1 pound **chicken breast or thighs**, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1/2 pound **beef or lamb**, cut into small pieces (optional)

### Spices
- 1 tablespoon **ground cumin**
- 1 tablespoon **ground coriander**
- 1 teaspoon **ground cinnamon**
- 1/2 teaspoon **ground cayenne pepper**
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

### Aromatics
- 2 medium **onions**, chopped
- 3 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 Scotch bonnet pepper (optional), chopped (use gloves when handling)

### Rice
- 2 cups **long-grain rice**

### Liquid
- 4 cups **chicken broth**
- 2 cups **water**
- 2 tablespoons **vegetable oil**

---

## Jollof Rice Sauce

Mix the following:

- 2 tablespoons **tomato paste**
- 1 tablespoon **grated ginger**
- 1 tablespoon **ground cumin**
- 1 teaspoon **salt**
- 1/4 teaspoon **black pepper**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Spices
In a small bowl, mix together the ground cumin, coriander, cinnamon, and cayenne pepper.

### 2. Sear the Chicken and Beef (if using)
Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken or beef and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from the pan and set aside.

### 3. Soften the Onions
Reduce the heat to medium and add the chopped onions to the pan. Cook, stirring occasionally, until they're softened and lightly browned, about 8-10 minutes.

### 4. Add Aromatics and Spices
Add the minced garlic and chopped Scotch bonnet pepper (if using) to the pan. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Stir in the spice mixture and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.

### 5. Add Rice, Broth, and Oil
Add the long-grain rice to the pan, stirring to coat with the oil and mix with the spices.
