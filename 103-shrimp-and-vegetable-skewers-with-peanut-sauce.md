# Shrimp and Vegetable Skewers with Peanut Sauce (Cambodian)

Brief description of the dish: A classic Cambodian street food made by marinating shrimp, vegetables, and chilies in a mixture of fish sauce, lime juice, and chili paste, then grilling on skewers.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat and Seafood
- 1 pound **large shrimp**, peeled and deveined
- 2 tablespoons **fish sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **lime juice**

### Vegetables
- 1 cup **bell peppers**, cut into large pieces
- 1 cup **onions**, cut into large pieces
- 1 cup **zucchini**, cut into large slices
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced

### Nuts and Seeds
- 2 tablespoons **peanuts**

### Spices and Seasonings
- 1 tablespoon **chili paste**
- 1 teaspoon **ground cumin**
- 1/2 teaspoon **salt**
- 1/4 teaspoon **black pepper**

---

## Peanut Sauce

Mix the following:

- 1/2 cup **peanut butter**
- 2 tablespoons **fish sauce**
- 2 tablespoons **lime juice**
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 tablespoon **chili paste**
- 1/4 teaspoon **salt**
- 1/4 teaspoon **black pepper**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the marinade
In a blender or food processor, combine fish sauce, lime juice, chili paste, cumin, salt, and black pepper. Blend until smooth.

### 2. Marinate the shrimp and vegetables
In a large bowl, combine the shrimp, bell peppers, onions, zucchini, garlic, peanuts, and marinade mixture. Mix well to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 2 hours.

### 3. Thread the skewers
Thread the marinated shrimp and vegetables onto skewers, leaving a little space between each piece.

### 4. Grill the skewers
Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Brush the skewers with oil and cook for 8-10 minutes per side, or until the shrimp are pink and cooked through.

---
