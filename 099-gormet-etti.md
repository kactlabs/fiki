# Gormet Etti (Ethiopian Braised Lamb Shanks)

Gormet Etti is a traditional Ethiopian dish made with slow-cooked lamb shanks in a rich and spicy stew, served with injera bread.

---

## Ingredients

### Protein
- 1.5 kg **lamb shanks**
- 2 **onions**, chopped
- 3 **garlic cloves**, minced
- 2 **teaspoons** **ground cumin**
- 1 **teaspoon** **ground coriander**
- 1 **teaspoon** **turmeric powder**
- 1 **teaspoon** **cayenne pepper**

### Aromatics
- 2 **carrots**, chopped
- 2 **celery stalks**, chopped
- 2 **bay leaves**
- 2 ** cloves**, whole

### Fat and Oil
- 4 **tablespoons** **vegetable oil**
- 2 **tablespoons** **clarified butter**

### Grains
- 1/2 cup **all-purpose flour**
- 2 cups **water**

---

## Gormet Etti Sauce (Berbere Spice)

Mix the following:

- 4 **tablespoons** **berbere spice mix**
- 2 **teaspoons** **ground cumin**
- 1 **teaspoon** **ground coriander**
- 1/2 **teaspoon** **cayenne pepper**

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Preparing the Lamb Shanks
Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Sear the lamb shanks until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes per side. Remove from pot and set aside.

### 2. Soaking the Berbere Spice Mix
In a small bowl, mix together berbere spice mix, cumin, coriander, and cayenne pepper. Add 1/4 cup of water to the mixture and stir until it forms a smooth paste.

### 3. Browning the Aromatics
Heat another tablespoon of vegetable oil in the same pot over medium heat. Sauté chopped onions, carrots, celery, bay leaves, and cloves until the vegetables are tender, about 8 minutes.
