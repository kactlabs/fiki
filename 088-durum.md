# Dürüm (Turkish Stuffed Sausage)

Brief description of the dish: Dürüm is a popular Turkish dish consisting of small, stuffed sausages typically filled with spiced meat and served in a pita bread with vegetables and sauces.

---

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 500g **ground lamb**
- 250g **ground beef**

### Spices and Herbs
- 2 tbsp **paprika**
- 1 tsp **cumin**
- 1 tsp **coriander**
- 1/2 tsp **cayenne pepper**
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

### Vegetables
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 carrot, grated
- 1 celery stalk, grated

### Sauce
- 2 tbsp **yogurt**
- 1 tsp **dried oregano**

---

## Dürüm Sauce (optional)

Mix the following:

- 1/4 cup **olive oil**
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 tsp **paprika**
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

---

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Preparing the Meat Filling
Combine ground lamb, ground beef, paprika, cumin, coriander, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper in a bowl. Mix well until just combined.

### 2. Cooking the Onion and Garlic Mixture
Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

### 3. Adding Vegetables to the Pan
Add grated carrot and celery stalk to the pan with the onion and garlic mixture. Cook for about 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently, until the vegetables start to soften.

### 4. Assembling the Dürüm
Using your hands, shape the meat filling into small sausages, about 5 cm in length. Wrap each sausage in a piece of aluminum foil or parchment paper and seal the edges by twisting the foil.

### 5. Cooking the Dürüm
Place the wrapped dürüm on a grill or grill pan over medium heat. Cook for about 5-7 minutes per side, until the meat is cooked through and the sausage is nicely browned.
