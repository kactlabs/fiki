# Palak Paneer (Indian Spinach and Cheese Curry)

Palak paneer is a popular Indian dish made with spinach, paneer (Indian cheese), and spices. It's often served as a main course or side dish in North Indian cuisine.

## Ingredients

### Vegetables
- 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)

### Spices and Seasonings
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
- Salt to taste

### Paneer
- 250 grams paneer, cut into small cubes

### Liquids
- 1 cup water
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Palak Paneer Sauce (if applicable)

Mix the following:

- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- Salt to taste

## Recipe Steps

### 1. Prepare the Spinach Puree
Combine spinach leaves, onion, garlic, and ginger in a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth. Heat ghee in a pan over medium heat. Add spinach puree and cook, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens.

### 2. Cook the Paneer
In a separate pan, melt ghee over medium heat. Add paneer cubes and cook until they're lightly browned on all sides.

### 3. Add Spices and Seasonings to Spinach Puree
Stir in cumin, coriander, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt into the spinach puree.

### 4. Combine Spinach Puree with Paneer
Add cooked paneer cubes to the spinach puree and stir to combine.

### 5. Add Water and Lemon Juice
Gradually add water and lemon juice to the pan, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens.

## Optional: Variation - Roasted Garlic

- To roast garlic, slice the top off a whole head of garlic, drizzle with olive oil, and wrap in foil. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 30 minutes or until soft. Squeeze roasted garlic into the spinach puree before adding it to the pan.
