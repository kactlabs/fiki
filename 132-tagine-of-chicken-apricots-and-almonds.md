# Tagine of Chicken, Apricots, and Almonds (Moroccan)
Traditional Moroccan dish made with chicken, apricots, and almonds in a flavorful tagine sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Proteins and Poultry
- 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

### Aromatics
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika (Ras el hanout)
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
- Salt and black pepper

### Vegetables
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 large carrots, peeled and sliced
- 2 celery stalks, sliced
- 1 can (14 oz) diced apricots
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

### Spices and Preserved Lemons
- 2 tablespoons preserved lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric

---

## Tagine Sauce (Dghaif)
Mix the following:

- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- Salt and black pepper
